Why donate to
HERoines Inc?

women to gather to discuss ALL issues that matter to them. We
also see the need to create and share content that can continue
these conversations and empower women even after these
events. Your contribution will allow us to continue to host events
and create content that will help cultivate stories from our
attendees that we hope to share with you.
